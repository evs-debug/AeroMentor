The Boeing 787 Dreamliner is a long-range wide-body airliner designed for medium- and long-haul operations.

The aircraft makes extensive use of composite materials, reducing weight and improving fuel efficiency.

The 787 is available in several variants, including the 787-8, 787-9, and 787-10. It is commonly powered by either General Electric GEnx engines or Rolls-Royce Trent 1000 engines.

The aircraft is known for improved passenger comfort, fuel efficiency, and long-range capabilities.
