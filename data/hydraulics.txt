Definition:
Aircraft hydraulic systems use pressurized fluid to operate critical aircraft components.

Functions:
- Flight controls
- Landing gear
- Brakes
- Steering systems

How It Works:
Hydraulic pumps generate pressure that is transmitted through fluid-filled lines to actuators.

Advantages:
- High force output
- Reliable operation
- Precise control

Importance:
Hydraulic systems are essential for controlling many aircraft functions safely and efficiently.
