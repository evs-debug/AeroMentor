The Airbus A320 is a narrow-body commercial airliner designed for short- and medium-haul routes.

It is one of the most successful commercial aircraft ever produced and is widely used by airlines around the world.

The aircraft typically seats between 140 and 180 passengers depending on configuration. It features a fly-by-wire flight control system, which replaces many traditional mechanical controls with electronic systems.

The A320 family is known for its fuel efficiency, reliability, and popularity among airlines.
