Definition:
The Airbus A380 is a double-deck wide-body airliner developed by Airbus.

Key Facts:
- Largest passenger airliner ever built.
- Can carry over 500 passengers in typical configurations.
- Designed for high-capacity routes between major airports.
- Powered by four turbofan engines.

Importance:
The A380 demonstrated Airbus' capability to compete in the ultra-large aircraft market and became an icon of modern aviation.
