Definition:
The Airbus A350 is a long-range wide-body commercial airliner designed for medium- and long-haul operations.

Specifications:
The A350 family includes the A350-900 and A350-1000 variants. It is designed for efficient long-distance travel.

Passenger Capacity:
Depending on configuration, the aircraft typically accommodates between 300 and 410 passengers.

Range:
The A350 can operate routes exceeding 15,000 kilometers depending on variant.

Engines:
The aircraft is powered by Rolls-Royce Trent XWB turbofan engines.

Fuel Efficiency:
Extensive use of composite materials and advanced aerodynamics improve fuel efficiency and reduce operating costs.

Importance:
The Airbus A350 is one of the most advanced and fuel-efficient long-haul airliners in service today.
