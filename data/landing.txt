Definition:
Landing is the phase of flight where an aircraft returns safely to the ground.

Procedure:
- Establish a stable approach.
- Align with the runway.
- Reduce descent rate before touchdown.
- Apply braking after landing.

Key Factors:
- Wind conditions.
- Runway condition.
- Aircraft weight.
- Visibility.

Importance:
Landing is one of the most demanding phases of flight and requires careful control and planning.
