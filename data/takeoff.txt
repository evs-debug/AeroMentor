Definition:
Takeoff is the phase of flight in which an aircraft accelerates along the runway and becomes airborne.

Procedure:
- Apply takeoff power.
- Accelerate along the runway.
- Reach rotation speed.
- Lift off and establish climb.

Key Factors:
- Aircraft weight.
- Runway length.
- Wind conditions.
- Aircraft performance.

Importance:
Takeoff is one of the most critical phases of flight and requires precise pilot performance.
